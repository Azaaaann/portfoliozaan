FAQ

Quick answers before you order.

What do I need to send before you start?

Share your video title, topic, preferred style, reference thumbnails, and any images or logo files you want included.

Which file formats will I receive?

You will receive high-quality JPG or PNG files suitable for YouTube, Instagram, and social media posting.

Can you match my existing branding?

Yes. The Standard and Premium plans are best when you want a consistent brand look across multiple thumbnails.

How do revisions work?

Revision count depends on the plan. Premium includes unlimited revisions until the design feels right for your project.
